Problem Statement

Design and build a secure, easy-to-use, affordable, transparent voting system that allows voters to verify their ballots. It should be reliably auditable to prevent voter fraud without impugning on voter privacy.

Specifications

Specifications based on Election Assistance Commissionâ€™s Voluntary Voting System Guidelines 2.0:

  • Open-source hardware and software
  • Protect voter privacy
  • Voter-verifiable ballots without trusting computer
  • Easy to audit paper record
  • Tamper-resistant equipment with minimal attack vectors
  • Access control mechanisms to prevent multiple votes
  • Detection and monitoring of tampering/unauthorized activity
  • Easy to use with minimal voter error
  • Affordable
